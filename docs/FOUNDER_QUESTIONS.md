# Founder Questions

Answer these before real backend and AI integration starts. Short answers are fine.

## Business Model

1. Is the first paid product B2C single-student pass, B2B consultant subscription, or both? Deferred.
2. What exact first paid action should be blocked by payment: OCR, master essay, tailored essay, PDF export, or university count? Deferred.
3. What price range are you considering for B2C and B2B? Deferred.
4. Should B2B customers buy seats, credits, students, or monthly usage? Deferred.

## Target Users

5. Who is the first buyer: student, parent, academy director, school counselor, or independent consultant?
6. Should the UI eventually support Korean, English, or both? Both Korean and English.
7. For Phase 1, should students self-serve or should consultants operate most workflows? Student self-service.

## Admissions Scope

8. Which exact universities are the first targets? Use top 13: SNU, Yonsei, Korea, Sogang, SKKU, Hanyang, Chung-Ang, Kyung Hee, HUFS, University of Seoul, Ewha, KAIST, POSTECH.
9. Which application year should the guideline database start with? 2026.
10. Are we supporting only 12-year and 3-year tracks, or also other overseas/Korean special cases? Start with 12-year and 3-year; keep expandable.
11. Should medical, dental, pharmacy, veterinary, and nursing all use the same medical branch? Yes.
12. Do you already have verified 2025 or 2026 guideline PDFs? Yes, and official PDFs can be found online.

## Data and Compliance

13. Will users upload passports, residence proof, family records, or only school documents? Do not collect sensitive identity documents unless needed for eligibility.
14. Do we need parent consent for minors? Yes. Under-14 users require legal representative consent.
15. Where should data be hosted: Korea, Singapore, US, or flexible? Prefer Korea.
16. How long should uploaded documents be retained? Until account deletion or deletion request, subject to legal retention needs.
17. Who can delete a student record? The account owner. Verified parent links can edit, but deletion ownership needs final legal/product confirmation.

## OCR

18. What countries and curriculums are most common in your expected documents? Korea-based users preparing overseas special admission; curriculum diversity still needs discovery.
19. Which documents matter first: transcript, graduation certificate, activity proof, passport, residence proof? Transcript first, then activity proof.
20. Are handwritten or scanned low-quality documents common? Expect mostly good PDFs.
21. Should consultants be able to manually correct OCR output before approval? Not Phase 1; student confirmation is enough.

## Essay Product

22. Do you want the master essay to be written in Korean, English, or both? Both.
23. Should the app generate Korean university-specific essays in Korean by default? Generate both Korean and English.
24. How strict should the system be when a student writes an unsupported claim in the story answer? Warn clearly that false claims can hurt admission, and block final submission until resolved.
25. Should consultants be allowed to override fact-check warnings? Yes, but log overrides.
26. Do you want a visible revision history for every essay? Yes.

## Consultant Workflow

27. Does each academy need multiple staff accounts? Use account links later rather than academy-first ownership.
28. Should consultants assign students to counselors? Deferred.
29. Should students and consultants comment inside the app? Good future community/Q&A direction.
30. Should final PDFs include academy branding from day one? Not Phase 1.

## AI Policy

31. Should AI outputs be labeled as drafts requiring human review? Do not over-label in user-facing copy.
32. Do you want to store every AI prompt and response for audit? Yes.
33. Should the app refuse to generate if confidence is too low? Do not block everything; require confirmation before using low-confidence facts.
34. What is the maximum acceptable hallucination rate for final tailored essays? 0% target.

## Launch

35. What is the target launch date? No rush.
36. Do you have 3-5 pilot students or consultants for testing? Yes.
37. What result would prove MVP success after the first month? Functional quality over revenue for now.
